    Chess Boom

    During the pandemic, chess saw a oom in its popularity. People are stuck at home with lots of free time. Online chess has proven to be a good option for those who are bored because you can play a game anyime you want. What may be a bigger factor was the release of a new series:The Queen's Gambit. The show is one of the most popular ever and is watched by people all over the world. The effects of the resurgence will be seen even when this is all over. References

    Why You Should Consider Playing

    There are many reasons why you should play chess. Above all, you should play it if it makes you happy and this goes for practically anything else. It can be a great way to spend your free time and it may provide you an escape for the real world. Not only that, chess can help imrpove your problem solving skills and develop your creativity. The magical journey all starts when you learn the rules and play some games. References

    What To Look Forward To

    Every 2 years, with an exception to this cycle because of the pandemic, the World Chess Championship takes place. Chess fans around the world look forward to this event. To avid chess fans, it will be interesting to see how and if Carlsen defends his title. With the match set to take place a year after it was supposed to, it will definitely have many on the edge of their seats. References
